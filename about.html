About Street Treasure Hunter

Welcome to Street Treasure Hunter, a community-driven platform dedicated to giving finding a second life for free items in your neighborhood.

One person's trash is another person's treasure.

Have you ever walked down the street and seen a perfectly good chair, a stack of books, or a vintage lamp left out with a "FREE" sign? We believe these items deserve a new home, not a landfill.
